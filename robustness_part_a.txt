First robustness layer

GARCH-filtered residual tail analysis
fit GARCH or t-GARCH to daily returns,
standardize residuals,
test whether standardized residuals still show fat tails,
compare model-implied horizon shocks with empirical historical shocks.





Second robustness layer

EVT / POT on filtered residuals
use EVT on the standardized residuals,
examine whether the far-left tail is heavier than normal/t assumptions imply,
especially for 99% shocks.
